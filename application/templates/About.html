Welcome to the AI Fairness Dashboard

In the rapidly evolving world of artificial intelligence, ensuring fairness in AI models is paramount. As these models influence an increasing number of decisions, the importance of transparency and equity cannot be understated.

This dashboard helps to analyze and visualize fairness in AI models using various definitions such as statistical parity, equal opportunity, and equalized odds.

We're committed to promoting fairness in AI and providing tools that empower users to understand and address potential biases. Through this dashboard, we aim to provide a clear and comprehensive view of how AI models perform across different fairness metrics.
